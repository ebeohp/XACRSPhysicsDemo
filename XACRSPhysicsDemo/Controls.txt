XACRS - XR Advanced Combat Robotics Simulator

This is a demo of a control drill where you control your bot to hit waypoints as efficiently as possible.


These are PC controls meant to mimic an RC controller with two joysticks.

W Key: Spin weapon
S Key: Stop Weapon

Arrow Keys: Drive the bot. These controls simulate realistic bot turning & handling. 
	Left/Right: Rotate the Bot. 
	Up/Down: Move forward and backward of the bot.
